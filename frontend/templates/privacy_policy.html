Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we handle your data on Lost & Found Platform.

1. Introduction

Lost & Found Platform values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it to provide our services.

2. Data Collection

We collect information when you report lost or found items, including:

  • Item name
  • Category (Phone, Wallet, Bag, Laptop, Keys, Other)
  • Location
  • Description
  • Date lost or found

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Match lost and found items
  • Display items on dashboards
  • Improve our services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to provide matching services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies for analytics and to improve site functionality. No personal data is sold.

7. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team at support@lostfound.com.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. All changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at support@lostfound.com.
